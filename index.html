Yes. We send you a snapshot of your site's speed and SEO scores plus the top issues, at no cost and with no obligation. It's how we show you we're worth talking to — the full deep-dive report is the paid step, only if you want it.

I'm not techy — will I understand the report?

That's the whole point of Wings. No jargon. We explain what each issue means, why it matters to your customers and sales, and what to do about it — in plain English.

Do I have to let you do the fixes?

Never. Take the report and fix things yourself or with your current developer. We're happy to help if you want us to, but there's no pressure and no lock-in.

What if I don't even have a website yet?

Perfect timing. We build fast, modern, mobile-first sites designed to be found on Google — for a fair fixed price. Just tell us about your business in the form below.

Do I need to be local to work with you?

Not at all. Every audit is done remotely, so we work with businesses wherever they are — right across Australia and beyond. If you've got a website, we can help.
